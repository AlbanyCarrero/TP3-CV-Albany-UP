About me

Born and raised in New York, USA. From a young age, I have always been passionate about music and literature, which led me to study philosophy and literature at Fordham University. In my music, I like to explore themes of love, melancholy, death and decay, often drawing inspiration from pop culture and film. I love creating characters and telling stories through my songs, and I hope my fans find comfort and connection in my music.
